Prepare for a MaiaEdge conference appearance.

Arguments provided: $ARGUMENTS

Invoke the event-intelligence skill in Mode 1 (Pre-Conference Prep). Research the event, build a prioritized target list from exhibitors/speakers, cross-reference with HubSpot, and produce a target briefing with per-company talking points.

Always ask the user:
1. Are we exhibiting, attending, or just monitoring this event?
2. Who from MaiaEdge is going? (Tim Lieto, Ken Cunningham, both, or other)
3. Any specific companies or segments to prioritize?

Use the conference-specific intelligence section of the event-intelligence skill to apply relevant talking points and key signals for the target conference.
