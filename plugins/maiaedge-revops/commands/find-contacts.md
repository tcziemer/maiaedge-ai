Find contacts at MaiaEdge target accounts.

Arguments provided: $ARGUMENTS

Invoke the contact-discovery skill based on what's requested:

- `[company name]` — Map existing HubSpot contacts at this account + identify persona gaps
- `coverage [company]` — Audit HubSpot contact coverage depth at this account
- `prospect [company]` — Search Apollo for new contacts to add at this account
- `gaps [segment]` — Analyze persona coverage gaps across all accounts in a segment
