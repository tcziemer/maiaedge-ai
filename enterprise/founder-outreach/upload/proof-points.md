PUBLIC REFERENCE - LinkedIn testimonial available

"If you are familiar with MegaPort and how it functions - imagine having that capability between providers who also have Path Border Controllers (PBCs) and the transport between can either be private fiber or Dedicated Internet Access. They're using MaiaEdge to connect with other operators, offer cloud on-ramps through partners, and they can do it over any transport - even DIA. His exact words were 'I'm getting one! You should get one!'"

## IENTC — Detailed Reference Customer Profile

**Segment:** Network Operator (VNO / Mobile Backhaul Provider)

**Location:** Mexico

**Scale & Scope:**
- **800+ cell towers** connected to network
- **20+ data centers** across Mexico
- VNO (Virtual Network Operator) providing mobile backhaul and connectivity services

**Deployment:**
- **3x PBC units:** ME-PBC-PCE-100G (36-month terms)
- **Pricing:** 35% discount from list price (strategic reference customer, first international, volume deal)
- **Quarterly billing:** Invoiced quarterly in advance
- **Total contract value:** $134,181 (TCV after discount, 36-month term)

**Key Business Context:**
- Mobile backhaul provider aggregating traffic from 800+ tower sites
- Needed to connect tower aggregation points to regional and national data centers
- Seeking federation capability with other carriers (INDATEL mentioned as strategic partner)
- International deployment (Mexico) created custom delivery terms (FCA Burlington, customer as importer of record)

**Use Cases Deployed:**
1. **Scale connectivity:** Aggregate 800+ tower sites to 20+ data centers with centralized PCE control
2. **Federation enabled:** Ready to peer with other carriers (INDATEL) through MaiaEdge federation
3. **Mobile backhaul optimization:** Deterministic paths for time-sensitive backhaul traffic
4. **Simplification:** Replace manual NNI setup processes with instant federation

**CEO Quote (Carlos Arguimbau, Founder & CEO):**
"We're very soon going to have a MaiaEdge device in our core network so you can peer with us through them. It's very very simple."

**What This Proves:**
- **Scale:** 800+ towers is the kind of massive scale that validates enterprise-ready platform
- **Mobile backhaul:** Proves MaiaEdge can handle real-world telecom infrastructure (not just colo/fiber)
- **International:** Validates that MaiaEdge works across geographies and regulatory environments
- **Federation mindset:** IENTC immediately understood federation value and sees it as competitive advantage
- **Reference for LatAm expansion:** Opens door to other Latin American VNOs and carriers
- **Commercial deal:** 35% discount for strategic value validates that pricing is flexible for key markets

**When to Reference IENTC:**
- Scale objections: "Can you handle hundreds of sites?" → "IENTC connects 800+ towers"
- Mobile backhaul prospects → IENTC is the proof point for this vertical
- International/Latin America expansion discussions
- Federation value proposition → IENTC CEO directly articulates federation intent
- Board/investor narratives → Scale proof + international validation + reference customer for LatAm

**Not for:**
- Colo prospects (different segment)
- Fiber-only operators (though fiber monetization applies)
- Western markets (IENTC is Mexico-based reference)
